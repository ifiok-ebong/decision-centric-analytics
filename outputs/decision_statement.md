# Decision Statement — Rivalytics (Decision-Centric Analysis)

## Decision (one sentence)
Prioritize **mid-tenure retention (4–12 months)** over onboarding improvements for the next quarter because it protects **more revenue at risk** while churn concentration is roughly split between early and mid.

## Decision-critical evidence (only)
- **Churn concentration:** Early 46.6% vs Mid 44.0% (near-tie).
- **Revenue at risk (MRR, prev month):** Early 798,415 vs Mid 1,407,419 → **Mid**.
- **Usage decay prior to churn (median):** Early -37.2% vs Mid -11.1%.
- **Support burden:** % churned accts with any tickets (last 3 months) — Early 11.0% vs Mid 37.4%.
